# Recommended Textbooks and Reading Materials

## üìö Required Textbooks

### 1. Numerical Linear Algebra
**Authors:** Lloyd N. Trefethen & David Bau III  
**Publisher:** SIAM, 1997  
**ISBN:** 978-0898713619

The primary textbook for numerical aspects of linear algebra. Excellent balance of theory and algorithms.

**Topics covered:**
- Matrix-vector multiplication
- QR factorization
- Least squares problems
- Eigenvalue algorithms
- SVD
- Iterative methods

---

### 2. Linear Algebra and Optimization for Machine Learning
**Author:** Charu C. Aggarwal  
**Publisher:** Springer, 2020  
**ISBN:** 978-3030403430

Modern treatment connecting linear algebra to machine learning applications.

**Topics covered:**
- Vector spaces and matrices
- Matrix decompositions
- Optimization fundamentals
- Gradient descent methods
- Neural networks

---

### 3. Convex Optimization
**Authors:** Stephen Boyd & Lieven Vandenberghe  
**Publisher:** Cambridge University Press, 2004  
**ISBN:** 978-0521833783

üì• **Free PDF available:** https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/

The definitive reference for convex optimization.

**Topics covered:**
- Convex sets and functions
- Convex optimization problems
- Duality
- Interior-point methods
- Applications

## üìù How to Read Technical Papers

1. **First pass (10 min):** Read title, abstract, introduction, conclusion
2. **Second pass (1 hour):** Read the whole paper, skip proofs
3. **Third pass (several hours):** Understand every detail, verify proofs
